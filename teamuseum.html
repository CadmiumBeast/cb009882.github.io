Ceylon Tea Museum

The ceylon Tea Museum is situated at the former Hantana tea factory which was built in 1925, located at the soutern side of kandy. Hantana was one of the first area where successfully opened up a tea cultivation afther the failure of the cofee enterprise. The factory that noe houses the Museum.


The four store building was abandoned for more than a decade before it was refurbished in 2001 by the Sri lanka tea board and the planters association of sri lanaka. The groud Floor houses 19th centuart colonial generators, rollers, dryers, fermentation tables, and sorting mashine and many more equipments. The secound floour houses the libary and the museum. The third floor has a shop whilst and the top floor contains resturants and tea rooms.
